Welcome to my homepage! My name is Jiaheng (Jeff) Hu. I am a 1st Computer Science PhD student at UT-Austin with a broad interest in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Robotics. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Peter Stone and Professor Roberto Martin-Martin. I hold a research M.S. degree in Robotics at Carnegie Mellon Univeristy (thesis), supervised by Professor Howie Choset. I graduated summa cum laude from Columbia University in 2020 with a B.S. in Computer Science, where I worked with Professor Peter Allen and Professor Tony Dear.
