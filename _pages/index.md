My name is Jiaheng Hu. I am a undergraduate student at [Columbia University](https://www.columbia.edu/) majoring in Computer Science, where I worked with Professor [Peter Allen](https://www.cs.columbia.edu/~allen/) and Professor [Tony Dear](http://www.cs.columbia.edu/~tdear/). I was also a summer intern at the Biorobotics lab of CMU, where I worked with Professor [Howie Choset](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~choset/bio.html).

My research interests is in intelligent control of robotic systems. I am still building my homepage.
